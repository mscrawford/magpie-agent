### 1. `q14_yield_crop` (equations.gms:14-16)

Calculates cellular crop yields by scaling calibrated baseline yields with the ratio of current technological change factor to the 1995 baseline:

```gams
q14_yield_crop(j2,kcr,w) ..
 vm_yld(j2,kcr,w) =e= sum(ct,i14_yields_calib(ct,j2,kcr,w)) *
                       vm_tau(j2,"crop") / sum((cell(i2,j2), supreg(h2,i2)), fm_tau1995(h2));
```

- Output: `vm_yld(j,kcr,w)` — crop yield (tDM/ha/yr) for cell j, crop kcr, water system w
- Scales calibrated baseline `i14_yields_calib` by the ratio of current tau `vm_tau` to 1995 baseline `fm_tau1995`
- Cited at `equations.gms:14-20` in module_14.md

### 2. `q14_yield_past` (equations.gms:35-39)

Calculates pasture yields using calibrated baseline, an exogenous management factor, and partial spillover from crop-sector technological change:

```gams
q14_yield_past(j2,w) ..
 vm_yld(j2,"pasture",w) =e=
 sum(ct,(i14_yields_calib(ct,j2,"pasture",w))
 * sum(cell(i2,j2),pm_past_mngmnt_factor(ct,i2)))
 * (1 + s14_yld_past_switch*(sum((cell(i2,j2), supreg(h2,i2)), pcm_tau(j2, "crop")/fm_tau1995(h2)) - 1));
```

- Output: `vm_yld(j,"pasture",w)` — pasture yield (tDM/ha/yr)
- Uses `pm_past_mngmnt_factor` (exogenous management factor from Module 70) and spillover from crop tau via `s14_yld_past_switch` (default 0.25)
- Notably uses `pcm_tau` (previous time step's tau) rather than current `vm_tau` — represents delayed knowledge transfer from crop to pasture
- Cited at `equations.gms:24-39` in module_14.md
